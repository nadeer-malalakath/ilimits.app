Guidance for Parents

Three practical steps to protect children from Chaturbate and the adult live streaming category — addressing both the viewing and broadcasting risk vectors, payment credential exposure, and the honest conversations that build lasting resilience.

🔒
Apply Comprehensive Browser and Network-Level Blocks Across Every Device Your Child Has Access to
Because Chaturbate's viewing access requires no account and no app, effective restriction must operate at the browser and network level across every internet-connected device a child can reach — including secondary phones, tablets, shared household computers and any device accessible on a home, school or public network. Use iLimits to apply category-level restrictions covering adult live streaming platforms, and enable safe-search settings on all browsers as a complementary measure. For home networks, consider applying restrictions at the router level so that all devices connected to the home network are covered simultaneously — including devices belonging to guests or family members that a child might temporarily access. Review and update restrictions periodically as children acquire new devices or access new networks.
💳
Remove Stored Payment Credentials From Shared Devices and Require Authentication for All Purchases
Review all devices your child has access to and remove stored payment methods — saved card details, digital wallets and in-app purchase authorisations. On shared devices, configure settings to require a PIN, password or biometric authentication for all purchases, preventing one-tap payment without the parent's involvement. Periodically review payment and bank statement histories for small unexplained transactions, which may indicate token purchases on adult platforms. Token purchases on Chaturbate are typically small individual amounts that can accumulate quickly, so looking for patterns of small charges rather than single large transactions is the more effective review approach. These steps are relevant even on devices where access has been restricted, as an independent layer of financial protection.
💬
Talk Honestly With Children About Both the Viewing and Broadcasting Risks of Live Adult Platforms
For older children and teenagers, a conversation that specifically addresses why adult live streaming platforms are harmful — including the broadcaster risk that is unique to platforms like Chaturbate — is one of the most durable forms of protection available. The fact that any user can go live, that adult viewers can send tips in exchange for on-camera acts, and that a child who broadcasts on such a platform is placing themselves in a deeply unsafe and potentially legally serious situation, are points that teenagers may not have considered. Frame the conversation around the child's safety and wellbeing rather than punishment, be specific about the platform's features rather than offering only general adult content warnings, and make clear that if a child has encountered this platform in any context they can come to you without fear. Technical controls and informed understanding together form the most complete and durable protection available.
