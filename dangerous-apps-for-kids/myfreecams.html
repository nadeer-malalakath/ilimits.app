Guidance for Parents

Three practical steps to protect your child from free-access live adult content platforms and build a foundation of open, honest conversation about responsible internet use and age-appropriate digital habits.

💬
Talk Openly With Children About What Live Adult Platforms Are and Why They Are Not Appropriate
Age-appropriate, honest conversation about live adult content platforms — what they are, how they work and why they are not suitable for children — is one of the most effective protective tools available to parents. Because MyFreeCams is discoverable through general searches without prior brand knowledge, children may encounter the platform without having deliberately sought out adult content. A calm, clear explanation of why live adult platforms are restricted — and why some websites that appear freely available are nonetheless off-limits — gives children a framework for recognising and avoiding similar content in environments where parental controls are not in place. Equally important is ensuring they know they can come to a parent without fear if they encounter something online that concerns or confuses them.
🌱
Encourage Healthy and Age-Appropriate Digital Habits
Help your child develop a thoughtful and balanced relationship with the internet by making it normal to talk openly about what they are watching online, which platforms they use and how they feel about what they encounter. Children who feel that their digital life is something they can share with a parent — rather than something they need to conceal — are significantly more resilient when they encounter content that is not appropriate for their age. Reinforce that restrictions on platforms like MyFreeCams are protections, not punishments, in the same way that age restrictions on films and television exist to protect children from content that is not suited to their stage of development.
🔒
Apply Browser-Level Controls, Secure Device Payments and Review All Devices Regularly
Apply iLimits restrictions consistently across every device your child has access to — including shared family computers and tablets — and review those restrictions periodically as children acquire new devices or access new networks. Because MyFreeCams is accessible through browsers without any installation, enabling content filtering and safe-search settings on all browsers used by children provides an additional layer of protection alongside iLimits. Also secure the payment methods stored on shared devices: MyFreeCams' interactive features require token purchases, and requiring biometric or PIN confirmation for all payments prevents a child from purchasing tokens on a shared device without parental awareness. The combination of technical controls, secured payment methods and open conversation provides the most complete and durable protection for your family.
